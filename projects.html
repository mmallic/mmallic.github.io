Project Title: Quick Recipe App
Overview:The Quick Recipe App uses an image-based UI for searching, sharing, creating, and saving liked recipes. The user has flexibility to search the recipe over the internet, save favorite recipes, and to share it with friends and family through social media. Using this app should enable the user to browse through top rated recipes based on the name of the ingredient put into the search box. The goal of this app is simple, to help the user make a quick and delightful meal with the ingredients available or by title of the recipe and to make an elaborated new meal

Project Title: E-Insurance Management System
Overview:Health Insurance Management System aims to leverage technology to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of service delivery within the health insurance domain. By offering an integrated platform for various stakeholders, including policyholders, healthcare providers, and insurance agents, the system seeks to automate business processes, improve communication, and elevate the overall user experience. The system will be a web-based application with a range of functionalities, including user registration, policy management, claims management and patient dashboard.

Project Title: Online Auction System
Overview: The Online Auction System is a mobile application designed to facilitate auction activities over the internet, enabling users from around the world to participate in buying and selling items through a competitive bidding process. This system aims to expand a firm's traditional on-site auction business into a global online marketplace like eBay
